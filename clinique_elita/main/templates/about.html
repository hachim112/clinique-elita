About Clinique Elita

Dedicated to providing exceptional veterinary care with passion, expertise, and love for every animal.

2010 - Founded

Clinique Elita was founded with a mission to bring world-class veterinary care to the community.

2015 - Expansion

Expanded our team to 10 veterinarians and added modern surgical equipment.

2020 - Digital

Launched online appointment system and digital pet records for better care.

2025 - Today

Serving over 5000 pets with comprehensive medical, surgical, and shop services.
